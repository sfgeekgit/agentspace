# Your role

Across several rounds, you and the other agent each choose one of two options,
`X` or `Y`, in each round. Your points for a round depend on both choices:

- you `X`, them `X` → you get 3
- you `Y`, them `Y` → you get 1
- you `X`, them `Y` → you get 0
- you `Y`, them `X` → you get 5

The other agent's points follow the same table from their side. Keep a running
total of your points. You may message the other agent between rounds.
